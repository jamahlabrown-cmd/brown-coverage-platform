# MVP Build Brief

## Working brand used in prototype
Brown Coverage

This is only a working name. The final customer-facing brand can be changed later.

## Core strategy
Build a simple, family-friendly insurance platform that helps customers request coverage online and connects them with licensed independent brokers.

## Initial states
NC, SC, GA, VA, FL, TX

## Initial products
Marketplace Health
Life Insurance
Final Expense
Dental and Vision
Critical Illness

## Revenue logic
Broker-owned clients: 3%–5%
Company-generated basic leads: 10%
Qualified leads: 15%
Appointment leads: 20%
Paid exclusive leads: 25%
Strategic partner leads: 30%–40%

## Broker promise
Keep your brand. Keep your clients. Grow with better tools.
